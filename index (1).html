About Our School \(@^0^@)/



Hello everyone! We are students from Santa Laurensia Junior High School. Santa Laurensia, a progressive Roman Catholic school founded in 1994, offers a bilingual, holistic education for primary and secondary students. With a diverse team of local and international teachers, the school fosters global awareness and cultural appreciation. It emphasizes lifelong learning, personal growth, and empowers students to navigate future global challenges.
